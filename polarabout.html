❆ About this project

POLARGLOBES centers around a group of (mostly) arctic animals and a now newly arrived human pilot, here to flip their once mundane lives upside down on the icy island they inhabit.
...Unbeknownst to them all, their day-to-day lives are being contained inside of a snowglobe.

⚠ Warning ⚠

This project contains sensitive themes and is intended for a mature audience. Please be wary of content warnings!
